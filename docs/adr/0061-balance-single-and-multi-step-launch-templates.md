# Balance single- and multi-step launch templates

The V1 Launch Collection will contain 16 single-step and four multi-step Form Templates. Contact will contain nine single-step templates and one intentionally conversational multi-step template; newsletter will contain five single-step templates; and quote request will contain two single-step and three multi-step templates.

This distribution keeps short public-facing contact and subscription tasks friction-light while allowing quote-request forms to group scope, service, budget, timeline, and contact details when that improves comprehension. The one multi-step contact concept must justify its progression through the visitor conversation rather than splitting a short form as an animation gimmick. Newsletter templates remain directly submittable from one view, although they may progressively reveal optional preferences without becoming a Step Flow.

Interaction Depth is independent of visual ambition. A single-step template may still be full-page, immersive, richly animated, or compositionally complex. Multi-step behavior will follow the existing shared Step Flow contract and will not introduce routing, URL state, separate schemas, or intermediate submission.
